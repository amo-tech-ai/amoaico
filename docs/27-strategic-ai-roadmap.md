### **Overview**

The Sunai platform has successfully established a robust, secure, and feature-complete foundation. The next stage of our growth is not just about adding features, but about embedding deeper, more powerful intelligence into the core of our product. This roadmap outlines a multi-phase strategy to leverage the full spectrum of the Gemini API, transforming our application from a tool into an indispensable AI partner for our users.

---

## **Phase 1: Hardening the Foundation (Current Focus)**

Before we build new wings, we ensure the engine is perfect. This phase is about implementing the non-negotiable engineering practices that guarantee stability, scalability, and a polished user experience.

*   **Key Initiatives:**
    *   **Production Hardening:** Implementing critical fixes from our system audit, including API rate limiting to manage costs and prevent abuse.
    *   **Developer Velocity:** Establishing a full CI/CD pipeline and comprehensive automated testing to allow us to build and ship new features faster and more safely.
    *   **User Experience Polish:** Implementing high-impact "quality of life" improvements like a global command palette (CMD+K), toast notifications, and enhanced skeleton loaders to make the app feel modern and responsive.

*   **Strategic Value:** This phase reduces technical debt, increases developer productivity, and improves user satisfaction, creating a stable launchpad for the advanced AI features to come.

---

## **Phase 2: Next-Generation AI Wizards (The Near Future)**

This is where we begin to unlock new markets and solve more complex user problems by building dedicated, AI-powered workflows. Each wizard is designed to showcase a specific, powerful capability of the Gemini API and act as a lead magnet for Sunai's core services.

| Wizard | Core Gemini Technology | Strategic Value & Use Case |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **AI Website & E-commerce Grader** | `googleSearch`, `Gemini Thinking` | **A Free Audit that Sells.** This tool analyzes a potential client's website and provides a scored report on "AI Readiness," recommending specific, high-value services Sunai can offer to improve their business. |
| **AI Social Media Campaign Planner** | `googleSearch`, `Text Generation` | **From Brand to Campaign in Minutes.** This wizard analyzes a client's brand and generates a custom social media strategy, including content ideas and draft posts, showcasing our expertise in AI-driven marketing. |
| **Interactive Co-pilot Demo Builder**| `Function Calling` | **A Tangible Preview of Power.** This sandbox allows potential clients to design and test a custom AI assistant for their own web app, providing a compelling, hands-on demonstration of our AI Web Application development services. |

---

## **Phase 3: The Vision - A Fully Integrated AI Co-pilot (The Long Term)**

Our ultimate goal is to evolve the platform into a proactive AI co-pilot that is deeply integrated into our users' workflows. This phase focuses on features that allow for greater personalization and automation.

*   **Key Initiatives:**
    *   **AI Content Strategist:** An agent that analyzes a user's business and competitors to automatically generate a full content marketing calendar, complete with blog post ideas, social media hooks, and SEO keywords.
    *   **"Ask My Data" Platform:** A powerful feature allowing users to upload their own proprietary data (CSVs, PDFs, etc.) and query it using natural language. This turns our platform into a personalized business intelligence tool.
    *   **Proactive AI Agents:** Moving from reactive tools to proactive assistants that can monitor data, identify opportunities or risks, and suggest actions to the user without being prompted.

*   **Strategic Value:** This phase completes our transformation from a collection of AI tools into a unified, intelligent platform that provides continuous, personalized value, driving long-term user retention and establishing a powerful competitive moat.
